Periods Tracker

Our aim is to develop a smartwatch application that assists users in monitoring and managing irregular menstrual cycles. This app will harness user-provided data spanning the first three months of their menstrual cycle and integrate a PPG sensor and integrated temperature sensor for predicting and detecting irregular periods. In cases of irregularities, the application will provide healthcare guidance, offering valuable information about nearby gynecologist hospitals to facilitate immediate consultation. Our aim is to empower users in efficiently managing their menstrual health and well-being through this innovative application.
